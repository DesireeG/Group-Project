Students with Disabilities

Many students are faced with one or more of a host of different disabilities, including, but not limited to:

  • Autism Sprectrum
  • Visual Impairness, including blindness
  • Deaf-Blindness
  • Deafness
  • Hearing Impairment
  • Learning Disabilities, such as dyslexia
  • Emotional Disturbance
  • Multiple Disabilities
  • Orthopedic Impairment
  • Other Health Impairments, including asthma, ADHD, diabetes, and epilepsy
  • Speech or Language Impairment
  • Traumatic Brain Injury
