Closing the Achievement Gap

An achievement gap is when one group of students significantly outperforms another group of students. The aim is to identify patterns and trends so that any discrepancies can be identified, addressed, and eliminated. These discrepancies could be due to a variety of factors, including:

    • Homelessness
      •
    • Substance abuse
      •
    • Other forms of abuse, including physical, sexual, or emotional
      •
    • Lack of community resources, such as organizations (including quality libraries, after-school programs)
